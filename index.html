Who is Nitesh?

Hi! I'm Nitesh Anand, a Computer Science engineer from Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT) with hands-on experience across mobile, web, and enterprise software development.

Currently a Software Engineer at Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) in Indore, I lead AI/ML analytical initiatives for the Government of Madhya Pradesh — coordinating with government stakeholders, writing functional documentation, user stories, and supporting UAT. I manage end-to-end project workflows on JIRA, design intuitive interfaces using Figma, and build both frontend and backend solutions — bringing a full-stack perspective to every problem I work on.

I've also interned at IIT Patna, IIT Palakkad and The Sparks Foundation, building iOS apps for medical ML, Android banking apps, and full-stack web platforms.
